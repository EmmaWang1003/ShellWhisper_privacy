Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ShellWhisper ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and in using our mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy applies to your use of the App and any related services.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Microphone Usage

The App requires access to your device's microphone to function. We record your voice input solely for the purpose of processing your requests and generating responses.

  • Real-time Processing: Audio data is processed in real-time.
  • Transitory Nature: Audio recordings are processed to generate text transcripts and are not permanently stored on our servers unless explicitly saved by you as a "Memory" in the App.

2.2 User-Generated Content

  • Memories: When you save a conversation as a memory, the text transcript and associated metadata (e.g., emotion tags, timestamps) are stored.
  • Storage: This data is stored locally on your device and synchronized via iCloud (CloudKit) if enabled. We do not have direct access to your private iCloud data.

2.3 Device and Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve app performance, including:

  • Device type and operating system version.
  • Crash reports and performance metrics.
  • Feature usage statistics (e.g., which themes are most popular).

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide core functionality:

3.1 AI Processing (OpenAI & DeepSeek)

  • Text Processing: To generate intelligent responses, text transcripts of your voice input are sent to third-party AI providers.
  • Providers:
    • OpenAI: Used for users in supported regions. OpenAI Privacy Policy
    • DeepSeek: Used for users in mainland China or as a fallback. DeepSeek Privacy Policy
  • Data Safety: We do not send personally identifiable information (PII) to these services. Only the text of your current conversation is transmitted.

3.2 Speech-to-Text (Apple Speech & OpenAI Whisper)

  • Voice Processing: For real-time transcription, audio data may be processed securely on-device via Apple's Speech framework, or transmitted to OpenAI's Whisper API for accurate recognition. Apple Privacy Policy | OpenAI Privacy Policy

3.3 Subscription Management (Apple StoreKit)

  • Payments: We use Apple's In-App Purchase system (StoreKit) to manage auto-renewable subscriptions.
  • Data: Apple processes your payments and validates your subscription status. We do not process or store your credit card information directly. Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID. Apple Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Data stored locally on your device remains there until you delete the App or clear the data.
  • iCloud Data: Data synced to iCloud is retained according to your Apple ID settings.
  • Server Logs: We do not maintain a central database of user conversations.

5. Your Rights

  • Access & Deletion: You can view and delete your saved memories directly within the App.
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke microphone access at any time in your device Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
